Project Title: Data Visualization using Python

Objective:
To visualize E-commerce sales data and derive business insights.

Tools Used:
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib

Charts Created:
1. Revenue by Product
2. Payment Method Distribution
3. Order Status Distribution
4. Referral Source Distribution
5. Quantity vs Total Price

Insights:
- Some products generate higher revenue than others.
- Customers use multiple payment methods.
- Most orders are successfully shipped or delivered.
- Different referral sources contribute differently.
- Higher quantity generally leads to higher total price.

Conclusion:
Data visualization helps identify sales trends and customer behavior patterns.
